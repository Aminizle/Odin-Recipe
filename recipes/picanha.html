Picanha

A Perfect Steak Every Time!

  • This recipe ensures a perfectly juicy Picanha steak every time!
  • Steaks can be cooked in the oven or on the grill.
  • A simple cut of beef is packed with flavor without a lot of fancy ingredients, sauces, or marinades.
  • This is a “foundation” recipe and truly, anyone can make great steaks.
  • I’ve included my favorite tips below to make a restaurant-worthy steak (and it costs much less at home too)!

Ingredients

  • Picanha Steak
  • Kosher salt.
  • Freshly ground coarse black pepper.
  • Garlic power.
  • Butter

Cooking steps

  1. dry brine Picanha the night before cooking with kosher salt.
  2. before cooking season with black pepper and garlic powder.
  3. In a pan put enough butter to baste the steak.
  4. set heat to medium.
  5. Cook steak to desired internal temp.
  6. let the steak rest for 10 min.
  7. Enjoy!
